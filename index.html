Turtle!

Turtles have been around for over 250 million years

  • Scientific Name: Testudines
  • Largest Turtle: Leatherback Sea Turtle
  • Average Lifespan: 20-30 years
  • Habitat: lakes, rivers, ponds, streams, wetlands, estuaries, and more.

Turtles are reptiles of the order Testudines. They have been around for over 250 million years. most of thier body is shielded by a special bony or cartilaginous shell developed from their ribs.
